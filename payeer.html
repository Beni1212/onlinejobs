PAYEER - PAYMENT PROCESSOR

What is Payeer?

Payeer is an online wallet (payment processor) that gives you the ability to shop online, send and receive money. The main advantage of Payeer is its multi-currency wallet that offers numerous options. Also, many forex sites and many online earning sites offer Payeer as a payment method. Payeer has been around since 2012. Personal as well as business users can access the services of this full-fledged payment system in over 200 countries, and it also works with many merchants and crypto-services. Payeer offers ordering a free MasterCard debit card (plastic as well as virtual).

Is Payeer Safe and Legal?

Payeer offers its financial services in the EU through Financa Private Limited Company. He received a license for the activity of virtual currency service from the Ministry of Economy and Communications (MEAC) of Estonia. This means that Payeer is a reliable processor that it is not a scam and that your funds are as secure as your documents. However, the experience of Payeer users shows that many complained that they could not withdraw funds via Visa / MasterCard. Payeer is safe and legal, but its withdrawal option has annoyed many users. If you are worried about how to transfer your money from Payeer to your card, one solution is to transfer from Payeer to PayPal, then from PayPal to the card.
